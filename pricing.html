Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial for the Business plan. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual contracts.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered nonprofit organizations and educational institutions.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time. We'll prorate your refund for the unused portion.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

Yes, our enterprise plan is fully customizable. Contact our sales team for a tailored solution.
